About Us

Many Years Experience In the Energy Industry

Sisonke Africa Engineering is a Black Economic Empowerment South African Construction Electrical Engineering Firm specializing in a wide range of services, including electrical infrastructure development, renewable energy installations, and substation construction and maintenance. With a team of experienced professionals, we provide innovative, high-quality solutions tailored to our clients' needs. Our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement is underscored by our pursuit of ISO 9001:2019 SABS Certification, ensuring top-tier service in the energy sector.

Sisonke Africa Engineering

Welcome to Sisonke Africa Engineering, your premier partner in cutting-edge electrical engineering and construction solutions. As a Black Economic Empowerment South African firm, we take immense pride in our rich legacy of delivering exceptional service across diverse sectors. Whether it’s renewable energy installations, healthcare facilities, or power generation, our expertise spans a wide array of industries, ensuring we can meet your specific needs with precision and innovation.

Our highly skilled team is the backbone of our success. With decades of combined experience and access to state-of-the-art technology, we are equipped to handle projects of any scale and complexity. From initial design to project management and final execution, our professionals are dedicated to transforming your vision into reality, always aiming to exceed your expectations.

At Sisonke Africa Engineering, our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We understand that each project is unique, which is why we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet your specific requirements. Our capabilities include substation construction, power line electrification, and maintenance of critical infrastructure, all delivered with the utmost attention to detail and quality.

Quality is not just a goal for us; it’s a standard. Our relentless pursuit of excellence is reflected in our ongoing efforts to achieve ISO 9001:2019 SABS Certification. This certification is a testament to our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of quality and continuous improvement in all our operations. We believe that by adhering to these standards, we can provide you with the best possible service and results.

Innovation and sustainability are at the core of our values. We are passionate about integrating cutting-edge solutions that not only meet today’s demands but also contribute to a sustainable future. By partnering with Sisonke Africa Engineering, you are choosing a company that is committed to making a positive impact on the environment and the communities we serve.

Partner with Sisonke Africa Engineering and experience unparalleled dedication, reliability, and technical excellence. Let us help you build a brighter, more sustainable future together. Whether you are looking to embark on a new project or need expert maintenance services, we are here to provide you with the highest level of service and support.
